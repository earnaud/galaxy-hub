The [Galaxy Career Center](/careers/) lists any open Galaxy related positions that we know about. There are currently 9 positions open in 6 countries.  Three of them have application deadlines this month:

* Limagrain, Chappes, France; **closes 11 December**
* RECETOX, Brno, Czechia; **closes 15 December**
* MeInBio, University of Freiburg, Freiburg, Germany; **closes 31 December**

The other six openings are "open until filled" which means they could close this month too (maybe because you fill one of them :-).

Note that there were three more positions that were posted in November, and that also closed in November.  If you don't want to miss those in the future, then subscribe to [@galaxyproject on Twitter](https://twitter.com/galaxyproject).
